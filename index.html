New Approach to Kids Education

Kidkinder is a Waldorf-inspired, tuition-free public charter high school guided by the Core Principles of Public Waldorf Education. Our program is rigorous, arts-integrated, culturally responsive, and relationship-centered. We celebrate the rich diversity and culture of Tucson and the Sonoran Desert region and engage our students in local and global sustainability.
